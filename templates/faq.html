YTBMP3.ORG - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do you download a video ? As explained in the menu tutorial, you first need to copy the
URL of the video you want to download from YouTube. Once this is done, you go to the site, paste
the URL into the search bar, and then you can click on "Convert". You will then be taken to another
page if everything goes according to plan. You have to choose what resolution you want to have if you
want a .mp4 file, if you are downloading a .mp3 video just tick a box, this will not influence the final
result. Then you choose the format, .mp3 or .mp4. And when you are ready, you can click on download,
you will find the downloaded file in your "Downloads" folder in no time. You're welcome ;))

What was the site designed with ? The site was designed with the basic web programming languages:
HTML, CSS, some Javascript, but it was also partly designed with the Python framework Flask.

My thumbnail is not displayed, is this normal ? Yes, it's possible that the thumbnail display may
have some bugs, but no problem since the upload works perfectly :))

Why do I have to select a pixel resolution when I want to download in mp3 ? Don't worry, what I choose
in the resolution part to download an mp3 file will not influence anything! For ease of use I have left it
visible during any type of download, but it doesn't change anything. So don't worry, choose randomly,
no problem!

How to change the theme ? You just have to click on the "Change Theme" button at the bottom of the site.
Your eyes will thank you !

If you have any questions, don't hesitate to contact me in the "Contact" part of the website footer
